Superstore Sales Analysis

Objective

Analyze business sales data to identify revenue trends, top-selling products, category performance, and regional sales performance using Power BI.

Tools Used

* Power BI
* Superstore Dataset

Key Metrics

* Total Orders
* Total Sales
* Total Quantity
* Total Profit

Dashboard Features

* Sales Trend Analysis
* Top-Selling Products
* Category Performance Analysis
* Regional Sales Performance
* Interactive Filters (Region and Date)

Key Insights

* Total sales increased steadily from 2015 to 2017.
* Technology generated the highest sales revenue.
* Canon products were among the top-performing products.
* The West region contributed significantly to total sales.

Recommendations

* Increase focus on high-performing technology products.
* Expand successful sales strategies in top-performing regions.
* Monitor discounts to maintain profitability.
* Prioritize inventory planning for top-selling products.
